About

Fragrance-free, African Soap from Burkina Faso is a natural remedy for all skin types. Our all-natural African soaps will help with a variety of skin problems such as acne, psoriasis, and dermatitis. Highly recommend pairing our African Soaps with Garner's Garden Moisturizing Body Oil and our Normalizing Face Oil in order to reduce the likelihood of dryness of the skin and to receive the maximum benefit of our African Soap. Ingredients: Honey, shea butter, osun (camwood), palm kernel oil, cocoa pod ash, palm bunch ash, aloe vera, lime juice, and water. (Note: When exposed to air it can develop a thin white-colored film, remove the white film by running it in water)
